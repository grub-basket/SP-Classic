# Stashpad Classic 0.102.19

Mobile fixes back-ported from mainline Stashpad — the low-risk, self-contained ones.

## Fixed (mobile)

- **First tap after typing lands on the right note.** After sending a note and
  dismissing the keyboard, the list's touch region could be left out of sync, so the
  first tap opened a note a full screen away. The list now re-syncs its tap targets
  once the keyboard settles.
- **Focused-note header no longer clips its buttons.** The action buttons (⋯ / kebab)
  could have their top edge shaved off on-device; the header now renders them fully.
- **Less flicker.** List and composer animations are disabled on mobile to cut the
  repaint flash on re-render, scroll, and selection changes.

## Not included

The larger mobile *layout* overhaul from mainline (bottom toolbars, nav controls in
the composer, 3-section rows, compact mode) is intentionally left out of this release:
it's a big structural change that needs on-device testing to ship safely.

## Compatibility

`minAppVersion` stays at **1.7.0** — installs on the current stable Obsidian desktop
release and older.
